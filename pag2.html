Guide for styles modern art

Pixel art

Pixel art is a form of digital art wherein images are created and edited at the pixel level using a graphics editing software. What defines pixel art is its unique visual style, where individual pixels serve as the building blocks that make up the image.

Font: Techopedia

Digital painting

Digital painting is an art form using computer software. An artist will operate their stylus like a brush or pencil to execute illustration and painting techniques within a software program using a computer screen or tablet as their digital canvas.

Font: Cgspectrum

Amateur photography

Use your smartphone to take pictures of beautiful places or exotic people or other things that make you smile. There are no bad angles or bad camera just unique concepts and points of view.

Fanarts

Fanart, which can be singular or plural, can be defined as graphic representations of various media forms that have numerous fans. Those who are significant fans of a certain character, show, fictional world or the like usually produce this work.

Font: MusicalExpert.org

Minimalist ilutrastion

A minimalist illustration reflects the idea that less is more. It takes complex concepts and ideas and finds the simplest (yet, efficient) ways to transmit them visually. Minimalism is trendy, but it is not exactly new since it typically follows aesthetic and cultural values as they evolve. Minimalism illustration allows designers and artists to focus on the subject's core by exploring the power of clean and clear lines and shapes.

Font: Imaginary Cloud

Art with recyclables

This type of art seeks to transform waste such as paper, cardboard, wood, glass, plastics, metals and rubber into works of art. The concept, therefore, goes beyond the conventional recycling of materials by creating objects that exceed the economic, cultural and social value of the original product.

Font: Iberdrola

Simple embroidery

You make drawing easier using thin lines, this type of art can be done on clothes, pictures or even curtains. It's a great exercise in creativity.

Simple sculptures

Use cold ceramics to make to shape the figures in your mind, develop your motor coordination and also your creativity. What is cold ceramics? Cold porcelain is a material used in crafting. As a type of clay, it can be molded into various shapes, making it a versatile material for different types of craft projects.
