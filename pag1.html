This Future is for everyone

The future of art isn't for genius.

The world thinks that artists are crazy or live in wonderland, many people don't think of possibly living for art, why agree that the artist has to be genius.

But everyone can turn their emotions, thoughts and memories into valuable works. We will continue to revolutionize our minds to bring to light the illustrious works that we hold only in our hearts.

"When you go out to paint, try to forget what objects you have before you, a tree, a house, a field, or whatever. Merely think, here is a little square of blue, here an oblong of pink, here a streak of yellow." - Claude Monet

Follow the next step. Carefully read how to unleash your creativity and then see the guide to the types of innovative arts.

Observe

Watch around and absorb the environment

People absorb the feelings, sensations and happenings around them. Organize the acquired information and try to identify how you feel about it.

Identify

Identify your type of art

Visit museums, galleries or look at the walls. What work most impacts you? What song makes you vibrate? What words run through your mind?

Free yourself

Shout out to the world through your art.

Express your occult through your works, release your voice without brakes, translate to people what is kept in your intimate.

Remember, there is no such thing as bad art, but there are people who don't understand art.
